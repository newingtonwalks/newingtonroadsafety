Newington Road in Fairfax County is Dangerous

Children, seniors, and others are forced to walk in the middle of the road. No sidewalks. No shoulders. No safety. Just cars and trucks speeding by.

  • Get Started

Fairfax County has described the road as "extremely dangerous"

A report by County staff in 2015 demonstrated the many ways Newington Road is an accident waiting to happen

  • Learn More

Adding a sidewalk to improve safety aligns with all of Fairfax County's strategic goals

The County Board of Supervisors in 2022 endorsed the Safe Streets for All Program to "address systemic transportation safety issues for people walking, biking and using other forms of active transportation."

  • Learn More

The Newington Road community wants safety

Over 70 households along Newington Road and the surrounding community have signed a petition asking the County to improve safety by building a sidewalk or paved pedestrian path

  • Learn More

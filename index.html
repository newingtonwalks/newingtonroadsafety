Over 100 families on and around Newington Road have signed a petition asking Fairfax County to build a sidewalk

  • Voice your support!

Safety is a top priority among residents as Newington Road (map) has no safe pedestrian infrastructure for children, seniors, dog walkers, runners, and anyone traveling without a vehicle

    Newington Road residents want to be able to safely:

  • Access nearby parks
  • Support local businesses
  • Visit their local library
  • Buy groceries from the local grocery store
  • Reach nearby public transit options
  • Add a missing link to connect to other sidewalks
  • Excercise, walk their dog, and go jogging
  • Let their children play outside

    • Fairfax County has described the road as "extremely dangerous"

      A recent report by Fairfax County staff outlined the many ways Newington Road is an accident waiting to happen noting:
  • "no pedestrian facilities"
  • "does not meet current safety, visibility, and design standards"
  • "extremely dangerous situation for bicyclists and pedestrians"

    • Voice your support!

    Adding a sidewalk to improve safety aligns with all of Fairfax County's strategic goals

    The County Board of Supervisors in 2022 endorsed the Safe Streets for All Program to "address systemic transportation safety issues for people walking, biking and using other forms of active transportation."

    • Support safety!

    The Newington Road community wants to safely walk to local parks

    Levelle Dupell Park is inaccessible due to the lack of a sidewalk to get there and Fairfax County Staff described the park as nearly impossible to reach the park without a car due to the lack of a sidewalk

    • Support access to parks!

    The Newington Road community wants to safely walk to support local businesses

    Several restaurants, delis, cafes, and a brewery are less than mile away but residents cannot safely walk to these businesses without a sidewalk

    • Support local businesses!

    The Newington Road community wants to safely access nearby stores

    Levelle Dupell Park, Kingstowne Library, Wegman's Supermarket, CVS Pharmacy, and more are located a short walk away but are inaccessible due to the lack of a sidewalk

    • Support pedestrian safety!

    The Newington Road community wants a safe, accessible, thriving community

    Nearly 100 households along Newington Road and the surrounding community have signed a petition asking the County to build sidewalk or paved pedestrian path
